Student Programs

Students participate in tutoring, workshops, and enrichment activities that provide them with a solid foundation in mathematics, as well as opportunities to explore higher-level mathematics. Students also practice problem-solving, ask questions, and stretch themselves beyond the classroom in pursuit of academic excellence. If you are a person that is interested in taking your mathematical knowledge to the next level, consider joining.
