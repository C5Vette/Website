About Us

Prateek Roy

President

Hi, I am Prateek Roy, and I have a strong passion for mathematics and problem solving. I am a member of the Minnesota All-State Math Team and had the opportunity to represent Minnesota at the National Science Olympiad, where I applied advanced math skills in competitive settings. I have also been a student in the University of Minnesota Talented Youth Mathematics Program (UMTYMP), where I have studied advanced topics beyond the standard curriculum.

Gabriel Rubin

Advanced Mathematics Tutor

Hi, I am Gabriel Rubin, I am an advanced mathematics student who completed AP Calculus BC as a sophomore, earning a perfect score of 5 on the exam. I am currently enrolled to be taking Multivariable Calculus at the University of Minnesota next year and have mastered mathematics through advanced calculus. In addition to my mathematical expertise, I have tutoring experience in history, biology, chemistry, and other subjects, bringing strong analytical skills and deep understanding to each discipline.

Aarav Shah

Advanced Mathematics Tutor

My name is Aarav Shah, and I am a current 9th grader at Eagan High School. Math has been something I have done all my life, something I am very passionate about to this day. It all started when I was 5, a little kid starting his first day of KUMON, something I would stick with till 8th grade. KUMON taught me many things, not just about math, it taught me skills I apply to this day, such as responsibility and time management, a key part of life and math. Outside of KUMON, I participated in Saturday Morning Math for 2 years through MATHCEP at the University of Minnesota and UMTYMP, the University of Minnesota's talented youth math program, which I'm in my 3rd year of and currently in calc 1. I also participated in middle school math league for 3 years, and now I currently volunteer my time to help 4th graders prepare for their math masters competition. Overall, math has played a big role in my life, showing me that hard work does really pay off and that anyone can really do math.
