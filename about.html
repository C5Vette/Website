About Us

Prateek Roy

Mathematics Lead

Hi, I am Prateek Roy, and I have a strong passion for mathematics and problem solving. I am a member of the Minnesota All-State Math Team and had the opportunity to represent Minnesota at the National Science Olympiad, where I applied advanced math skills in competitive settings. I have also been a student in the University of Minnesota Talented Youth Mathematics Program (UMTYMP), where I have studied advanced topics beyond the standard curriculum.
