Our Mission

The goal of Mu Alpha Theta is to promote the value of excellence in the field of mathematics by developing leadership, service, and academic achievement. The goal of our local chapter is to increase the availability of high-quality mathematical education by reaching out to local communities by providing workshops and practices. Most importantly, we aim to provide students from thrid world countries with content through online education.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Be part of our mission to transform education and change lives. Whether you're looking to volunteer or seeking support, we welcome you to our community.
